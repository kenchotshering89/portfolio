Project Overview

CordyChain is an innovative blockchain-based platform designed to bring transparency and traceability to the Cordyceps harvesting industry in Bhutan. The system tracks Cordyceps from the point of harvest through the supply chain until they reach the marketplace, ensuring authenticity and preventing fraud.

By leveraging Ethereum smart contracts, CordyChain creates an immutable record of each harvest, providing regulators, harvesters, and buyers with a trustworthy system that verifies the origin and journey of these valuable medicinal fungi.

Technical Implementation

The platform was built on the Ethereum blockchain with smart contracts handling the core business logic for harvest registration, quality verification, and transaction processing. The backend services provide REST APIs for mobile applications used by harvesters and web interfaces for regulators and buyers.
