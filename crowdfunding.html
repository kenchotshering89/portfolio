Project Overview

A revolutionary blockchain-based investment platform that democratizes startup funding by enabling fractional ownership through tokenized equity. The platform eliminates traditional barriers to startup investment, allowing anyone to invest in promising ventures with complete transparency and security.

Built on Ethereum with advanced smart contracts, the platform automates investment processes, manages equity distribution, and ensures regulatory compliance while providing real-time tracking of investment performance and startup milestones.

Technical Implementation

The platform leverages Ethereum smart contracts for core investment logic, IPFS for decentralized document storage, and a React-based frontend for seamless user experience. The architecture supports multiple investment models including SAFE agreements, equity rounds, and revenue-sharing tokens.
